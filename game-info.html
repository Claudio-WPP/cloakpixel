Q: What are the basics of the game?
Look around at your colleagues. Are they faithful? Are they a traitor? Eliminate the traitors in banishment opportunities. Be careful, as traitors can also 'murder' you and remove you from the game.
Q: How were the Traitors selected?
Traitors were selected completely at random using a random generator.
Q: What do we gain from doing tasks or games?
You are as a whole TPO team working to collect Cloak Pixels in in-game opportunities - each represents 1 hour off work. Stay in the game until the end for a chance to win your share of the 72 Cloak Pixel.
Q: Is there any way to protect myself from murders? In the TV show they have Shields
Yes, we have those too! Shields can be won during games or in bonus tasks.

Shields won during games are in play for the next murder decision, which happens after any banishments.

If a bonus shield becomes available 10 minutes before a banishment, it will protect against the following murder decision, as the traitors will have already locked in their current murder choice.
